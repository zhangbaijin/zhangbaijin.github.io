Biography

I am currently a PhD student in SJTU, under supervised by Pro.Chaochen Gu and Pro.Shanying Zhu , and also under supervised by Tang Hao.
I received my Master in Nanjing University Of Posts And Telecommunications, 2021. Now, my research interests focus on Computer Vision, especially low-level vision tasks, Large language model, Multimodal Large model.
