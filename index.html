👤 About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Chaochen Gu and Prof. Hao Tang.

My research focuses on large vision-language models, with emphasis on:

  • Understanding and mitigating hallucination in multimodal reasoning
  • Optimizing positional encoding for long-context understanding
  • Efficient attention mechanisms and information flow analysis
  • Embodied intelligence and real-world multimodal applications

Prior to SJTU, I received my M.Eng. from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, and worked as a Research Intern at Alibaba Cloud (2024-2025).
